01 /

pre-baked image

Ubuntu 22.04 base with OBS, noalbs, SRT/SLS, Xorg, Openbox, and x11vnc pre-installed. Flash it, boot it, and skip the setup marathon.

02 /

first-boot TUI

A curses wizard collects stream key, platform, WiFi, SSH key, and VNC password with sane defaults. It writes everything once and stays out of your way.

03 /

stream in minutes

NVIDIA hardware acceleration and battle-tested transport protocols are configured out of the box. The box boots fast and goes live without ceremony.
